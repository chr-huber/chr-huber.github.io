application procedure

Researchers from around the world can apply to be part of CREME.

The project coordinators will pre-screen applications for accordance with the researcher conditions outlined below and will invite all eligible research teams (RTs) to submit a proposal for a research design.

After successful application, all eligible RTs are invited to submit a proposal. Up to 40 RTs are then randomly selected to be part of CREME and to complete following stages: programming and implementation of the experiment, peer evaluation of other design proposals, and data analysis. Writing of the paper is done by the project coordinators. Learn more about the project outline and about the tasks for all RTs.

researcher conditions

To be considered for this project, research teams (RTs) have to follow the following researcher conditions:

  • maximum of two people per RT
  • at least one team member needs a PhD in Economics, Psychology or a related field
  • at least one team member needs one experimental study that is either published (accepted for publication) or a working paper
  • RTs have to briefly justify why they consider themselves eligible for the study
Borderline criteria will be voted on by the project coordinators (majority rule).
